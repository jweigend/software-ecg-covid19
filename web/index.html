What is Software ECG?

Software ECG (EKG in German) is a powerful time series analysis tool developed by Johannes Weigend and the team at QAware GmbH. The name stands for "Elektrokardiogramm" ‚Äì just like a medical ECG shows the heartbeat of a patient, Software ECG shows the "heartbeat" of your data.

This COVID-19 Edition is specifically designed to visualize and analyze pandemic data, helping researchers, data scientists, and curious minds explore the global impact of COVID-19 through interactive visualizations.
